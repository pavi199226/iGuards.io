About


iGuards security and facility management solution is designed to help security agency's to ease their operations and increase their revenue through streamlining their process. iGuards help the organization in increase project efficiency, profit and accountability by streamlining and mobilizing project communication and documentation. We have developed an online web application specifically for the security guard and facility management companies. There is a lot of paper work and excel work is done in these companies every month, To ease the operations we have tailored made our solution that would cater to all security guard and facility management companies.
